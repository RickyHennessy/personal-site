About Me

I'm Ricky Hennessy

Hi! My name is Ricky Hennessy. I am a creative data scientist with a passion for connecting data science and AI to true human needs. I lead the Designed Intelligence team at Fjord, where we combine data science and design thinking to design, prototype, and deliver data-driven products and services to our clients.


Some of my previous clients include The NFL, USAA, Lockheed Martin, Boeing, The State of California, The State of Texas, TIAA, BP, and LG. Prior to Fjord, I worked as a data scientist at Frog. I have Ph.D. in biomedical engineering from The University of Texas at Austin.
